Tips For You

1 - Use non-stick cookware: This helps reduce the amount of oil used and prevents food from sticking.

2 - Replace some of the oil with other liquids, such as lemon juice, vinegar, or water.

3 - Use a small amount of oil: Spray oil with a spray bottle instead of pouring or brushing it on.

4 - Maintain kitchen hygiene: Keep cooking surfaces and utensils clean to prevent the spread of bacteri.

5 - Wash your hands thoroughly before and after handling different foods, especially meat and poultry.

6 - Use separate cutting boards for meat, poultry, and vegetables to avoid cross-contamination.

7 - Ensure meat and poultry are thoroughly cooked to kill any harmful bacteria.

8 - Do not thaw frozen food at room temperature: Use the refrigerator or microwave.

9 - Use low-fat dairy products in cooking to add flavor and texture without adding a lot of fat.
